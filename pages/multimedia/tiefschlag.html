Tiefschlag

'Tiefschlag' is a performance lecture inspired by the story of the Holocaust boxers in the concentration camps. Through physical and digital space, spatial and sound installation and narration, _.mootpoint retells the little known stories of imprisoned boxers during the nazi era.

Conceptualized by _.mootpoint: Fanis Gioles (Multimedia, Sound, Interractive Installation), Anthoula Bourna (Space, Installation), Maria (Prerfomance Lecture), Dennis (Dramaturgy, Multimedia)
