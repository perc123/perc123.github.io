rocket rhapsody

Rocket Rhapsody was an interactive multimedia project in collaboration with HTW Berlin. During the Rocket Rhapsody concert of HTW-Tonkollektiv the audience had the opportunity to actively form the set of the concert by applying elements on an application, control their movement and their appearance duration.
