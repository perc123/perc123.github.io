elsewhere

"Elsewhere" is a captivating music-theatrical performance that explores the realm of sound through an array of percussion instruments. Drawing inspiration from the avant-garde compositions of John Cage, Vinko Globokar, Pierluigi Billone, and Giacinto Scelsi, the performance takes the audience on an auditory voyage that transcends conventional boundaries.
