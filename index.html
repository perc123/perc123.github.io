about

fanis gioles is a sound and multimedia artist, researcher, performer and percussion player. He has studied physics and experimental percussion, worlds that were combined after a long journey through mathematical logic, relativity, quantum physics, baroque aesthetics, complexity, musique concrète and electronic music. He is very active in improvisation, music theater, sound installations and performance, he is one of the founders and artistic directors of the multidisciplinary network "_.mootpoint" and his works have been presented in many places in and outside Europe. His artistic practices vary from self-made instruments, multichannel soundscapes and noise improvisation and mixed reality worlds. He was strongly influenced by C. Monske (discipline), K. Theodorakos (aesthetics), M. Stahnke (ethics) and T. Adachi (boundaries). As an artist he presented his works at festivals worldwide (Darmstädter Ferien Kurse, International Ensemble Modern Akademie, OPUS IX, San Marino New Music Festival), he has been a resident at Amplify Berlin and at ADATA AiR in Plovdiv - European Capital of Culture 2019 and the is the 2017 DAAD laureate in Hambung

For bookings or collaborations please send an email at fanis.gioles@gmail.com
